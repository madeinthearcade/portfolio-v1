Hey, I’m Peter,
Web Designer & Front End Developer based in Kent, UK – I create digital solutions to help shape the web today.

Currently working for a web design agency based in Maidstone, I tackle a broad variety of projects for different types of businesses. From informative to ecommerce based solutions, I’ve taken on multiple clients and shared my visions of a better world wide web.

“Intuitive design is how we give the user new superpowers.”

With my years of experience in the industry, I create beautifully crafted responsive websites with my distinctive strategic thinking. I like constantly putting concepts and ideas to the test and emphasize values that meet user needs as well as business objectives.

My one goal is to create & innovate.
